Dal-Rice

Dal rice is a popular and nutritious Indian dish consisting of dal cooked with aromatic spices, served alongside steamed rice. This wholesome combination provides a balanced source of protein, carbohydrates, and essential nutrients.

Chapati-Sabji

Chapati is a traditional Indian flatbread made from whole wheat flour. It is kneaded into a soft dough, rolled into thin rounds, and cooked on a hot griddle. This unleavened bread is a staple in Indian cuisine, serving as to various curries and dishes.
